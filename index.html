About me

Hey there! My name is Michael Smith! I am an advocate of health and fitness.
As a child, I struggled with obesity,
was enabled to eat all sorts of junk food,
and in that result, I was over 300 pounds!
At age 16, I was diagnosed with Type 2 Diabetes and was in utter disbelief.
I couldn't handle it anymore, and I decided to make my life change for the better.

I decided to go get a job to earn some money so
I can start getting weights and start a healthier lifestyle.
I started changing out my sodas with 0-sugared flavored water,
Candies and other sugared items into produce and healthier meats.

Throughout the course of 10 years, I have gotten my degree in
Health and Physical Education, Kinesiology and Excercise Science
At the University of CA San Diego. With that degree,
I have helped countless of people who were in my situation lose weight
and started helping them with diets and living healthier lives!

If you are ready to start today, you can always hit me up on my socials,
Or book an appointment by looking at the bottom of the page for
more info!
